Enjoy the roomy upper-level great room both day and night with vaulted ceilings, a living area with a gas fireplace, a kitchen area with a breakfast bar, a dining area with sound views, and a half bath. The living area is equipped with entertainment for all ages, such as movies, board games, puzzles, toys, an XBox, and books.

The 2nd level has all of the bedrooms, each with water views and TV/DVD. Plus, the hammock on the shaded desk just outside is the perfect place for an afternoon nap. The washer and dryer are conveniently located on this level as well.

The first floor features a covered carport, an enclosed outside shower with a dressing area, board storage, and a fish cleaning station. Around back, there is a private entrance to the sound that makes it safe and easy to kayak, stand-up paddleboard, etc.

Whatever your reason for coming to OBX -- star gazing, sunbathing, windsurfing, kiteboarding, beach combing, fishing, crabbing, or maybe some of each -- it is the perfect vacation destination.
